About the binaries

This is a pre-release of the Mac OS X port of JuceOPLVSTi together with the Windows releases. The Mac version is only for VST at the moment but the AU version is coming soon.

What is it?

The OPL2 is a simple 2-operator digital FM sound chip from the late 80s, used in PC sound cards like the Adlib and original Sound Blaster. This VST instrument allows you to program an emulated OPL2 chip.

Demo on YouTube (music from PC version of Syndicate, tracked in Renoise).
